Who Are We?

Hello and welcome! We are Malcolm and Valerie Anowma, and this is our company Cool in Shades. We are passionate about sun safety and protection which is why we specialise in selling sunglasses, a vital accessory for any time in the sun. We are also proud to be co-founded by the government's SunSmart program. We have worked together since the infamous 'slip, slop, slap' of 1981. Ever since then, we have provided sun safety tips, products and services.



Slip Slop Slap Seek
and Slide!

The Australian Sun is known for its extreme heat. This is both a blessing and a curse. Why? Well exposure to the sun has many fundamental benefits. Unfortunately, too much sun is bad for you. That is why Australia is the skin cancer capital of the world. So that is why we are here. Cool in Shades believe in helping you be sun safe. Ever since the 1980's when we first started working with the government we have been increasing our stock from more than sunglasses to help you slip, slop, slap, seek and slide. For more information about sun safety refer to government websites.
